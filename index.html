Automatic Water Level Controller

Instrumentation & Automation Project

Project Overview

This project involves designing and implementing an automatic water level control system for domestic and industrial applications. The system automatically controls the operation of water pumps based on the water level in overhead tanks or reservoirs.

Key Features

  • Automatic pump control based on water level
  • Prevents dry running of motors (motor protection)
  • Eliminates water overflow and wastage
  • Energy efficient operation
  • Visual and audible indicators for different water levels
  • Manual override option

Technical Specifications

  • Sensors Used: Float switches or conductive probes
  • Control Unit: Relay-based logic controller
  • Power Supply: 230V AC mains with step-down transformer
  • Indicator Lights: LED indicators for different water levels
  • Protection: Overload and short-circuit protection
  • Enclosure: IP-rated waterproof enclosure

How It Works

The system uses multiple sensors placed at different water levels in the tank. When water level drops below the minimum threshold, the controller activates the pump. When water reaches the maximum level, the controller turns off the pump automatically. Intermediate sensors provide indication of water levels.

Applications

  • Residential water tank management
  • Industrial water reservoirs
  • Agriculture irrigation systems
  • Swimming pool water level control
  • Boiler feed water control
